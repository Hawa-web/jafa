DOMESTIC TRANSPORTATION

As pioneers in this segment of the inland transportation series in Kenya, we take pride in offering the large industrial houses a very cost effective and complete distribution system for their products within Kenya. By providing containerized inland transportation on dedicated rail service we cater to bulk movement of high density as well as voluminous cargo. This system not only brings about considerable cost savings but also safe guards the cargo against pilferage and theft. Our Transportation service for all requirements related to Imports, Exports & Freight Forwarding in general.

The Transport Division, with its own fleet of Trailers ensures safe and time bound deliveries of Inland Cargo in Kenya and within the East African region. The Consultancy Divisions, with the most experience staff in this field, solves all the problems and answers all queries related to the Customs formalities and procedures. Warehousing facilities are provided whenever they are needed and help extended in doing so in the best manner according to requirements. Jaffery Island Coast Links Ltd stands miles apart from the numerous Freight Management Organizations dotting the country and serving the most distinguished Clients.
