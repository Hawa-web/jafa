LCL CONSOLIDATION

We offer a weekly consolidation service for all LCL shipments from Kenya. We will continuously endeavor to consolidate direct containers to individual regions thereby reducing transit times and costs. We are able to provide a fail proof inbound and outbound consolidation service to our exporters. Where ever your consignees are we will deliver right though to their door. A remote country in Eastern Europe or the smallest island in the Caribbean, we will deliver without much trouble to you.

Maximizing efficiency and minimizing costs in shipping doesn't always require filling an entire container. At Jaffery Island Coast Links Ltd, our Less-than-Container Load (LCL) consolidation services offer a smart and cost-effective solution for businesses that need to ship smaller quantities of cargo. LCL consolidation allows multiple shipments from different clients to be combined into a single container. This means you only pay for the space your cargo occupies, making it an ideal option for businesses looking to ship smaller loads without the expense of booking a full container. By sharing container space with other shipments, you benefit from reduced costs while still enjoying the reliability and security of ocean freight.
